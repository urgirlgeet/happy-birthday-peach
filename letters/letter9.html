Aaj thoda future ki baat karte hain. Where do we see ourselves in idk, 10-15 years types. Just soft glimpses, from my mind to yours, the kind that come to me randomly when Im smiling to myself, thinking, damnnn I do really want this with him.

I keep imagining us as this annoyingly perfect double income, no kids (well wait I will get to that later) household. Both of us doing our own thing, working hard, building our lives, and then coming back to each other at the end of the day like, ‘I’m home honey’, ‘Tell me about your day love’, ‘I cooked you this amazing dinner my heart’. Not in a dramatic way, just in that quiet, everyday kind of love. The kind where even sitting in the same room doing our own work feels enough.

And like obviouslyyyy, we’re going to be stinking rich, like unnecessarily rich, like itne paise toh kisi ko chahiye bhi nahi hote rich. The kind where we don’t check prices before ordering, where we randomly book trips just because we feel like it, where we spoil each other for no reason at all. But the real chaos? Our house. Full. Of. Pets. Our kids, not human kids but real fur babies. Like genuinely no control. Me with my cats, plural ofcourse, because do we really think we’re stopping at one? You with your big dog that you’ve always wanted, probably the softest giant ever who thinks he’s a baby. And then bunnies hopping around like they own the place. Our house is going to be less of a house and more of a fluffy zoo. There will be fur everywhere and zero personal space, such an ideal household if you ask me. Perfect happiness. I can already imagine random evenings. You on the couch, one cat on your lap, the dog lying dramatically on the floor, me sitting next to you complaining about something stupid, and you half listening but still pulling me closer. Nothing big, nothing special, but everything at the same time.

And then there’s us travelling. Seeing the world together. Different places, different versions of us. Getting lost in random streets, trying new food (we are for sure going to try food blogging at some point), laughing at dumb things, taking pictures we’ll look back at years later (I am going to print everything out and fill so many albums). But the best part? No matter where we go, no matter how far, home will always just be you, like I won’t need a place to feel settled, I’ll just need your arms around me. That’s my favourite part of this whole future. Not the money, not even the travel, not the house full of animals (okay maybe that). It’s the fact that in every version of it, its us, choosing each other at the end of every day.

Together we will work for good cause always, we will be the change we want to see in this world. With you, I know it’s never going to be in vain, we will actually make a difference, we will make this world a better place to live in, for us, for people we love and everyone else.

And then maybe one day, when we’re tired of the fast life, we’ll slow it all down. A little farmhouse somewhere far from all the noise. Mornings that don’t start with alarms, evenings that don’t feel rushed. More animals (because clearly we won’t learn, not that we have to, it’s a good thing to keep animals and love them), fresh air and happiness. Just us, existing peacefully after everything we’ve built together.

I don’t think I dream about the future in big, complicated ways anymore. I just see pieces. Small, soft moments stitched together. And every single one of them has you in it, so wherever life takes us, however it turns out, I just know one thing for sure, I want all my tomorrows to have you in them.

I love you peach, now and in all possibilities of any kind of future with you.

Happy almost birthday love!!

Yours forever,
