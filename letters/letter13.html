8, too real now!!

My bacha,

Not writing to you aaj, writing to baby kapeesh. The version of you who had no idea what was coming, who didn’t know the kind of person he was going to become, the kind of love he was going to have.

“You’re going to grow into someone amazing.” Not just in the obvious ways, not just in achievements or things people can see. But in the way you carry yourself, the way you treat people, the way your heart stays so soft even when life isn’t. You’re going to become someone people feel safe around, someone they trust, someone they love.

“You’ll be loved by everyone around you.” And not in a loud, attention-seeking way. But in the quiet, genuine way where people just know you’re a good person. Where your presence feels comforting, where your absence is noticed. You won’t even realize how many people care about you but they will.

“You will be smart and an all-rounder person.” The kind who can do a little bit of everything. The kind who keeps learning, growing, understanding the world in his own calm way. You won’t always see it, you won’t always give yourself enough credit, but trust me, you’re going to be so much more capable than you think. (but obviously you will always stay my same ol’ drummer boy)

“You will learn new things every single day.” Not just from books or work, but from life, from people, from experiences. You’ll grow in ways you didn’t even know were possible. And even on days when it feels slow or confusing, you’ll still be moving forward.

“You will be cats’ favourite person.” This one is very important. Because somehow, every soft little fur baby will just know that you’re safe. That you’re gentle, kind, loving. Yuki knew it, Tsuki knows it, and every cat that ever meets you will know it too. And honestly, I think that says everything about the kind of person you are.

“There will always be things you wish you could do differently, things you wish you could change.” And that’s okay. You won’t be perfect, life won’t be perfect. But even with all of that, you’ll be happy, constant happiness, the real kind. The kind that sits quietly in your life and makes everything feel worth it.

And then, the most important thing (at least to me). “You’re going to be the best boyfriend in the entire universe.” The kind who listens, who understands, who stays. The kind who loves not just in easy moments, but in the hard ones too. The kind who makes someone feel safe, valued and completely loved. “And you’re going to have the prettiest girlfriend ever.” Hehehe, obviously. That was non-negotiable. But more than that, you’re going to have someone who loves you in a way that feels like home. Someone who sees you for everything you are and still chooses you, every single day.

I think if younger you heard all this, he wouldn’t fully believe it. He wouldn’t know how it all comes together. But I do because I get to see you now. And you turned out even better than anything I could’ve told him.

Yours since forever,

Geet
