My bestfriend,

Somebody, Shah Rukh Khan, once said, ‘Pyaar dosti hai’ and I think he’s onto something here. You became my boyfriend on 27 November, yes pretty day but you have been becoming my bestfriend since our stars aligned peach. And I think that’s what makes us, us. Because before anything else, beneath everything else, we are built on something so real and so steady, friendship. Friendship is the basis of our love.

Yes, I am your girlfriend, and of course I want to look pretty to impress you. I want you to look at me and think, that’s my girl. But at the same time, you are my best friend, and with you, I’m not scared to look a little homeless either. I want you to admire me for my smartness, but I also love that you laugh at me, with me and sometimes because of me. I want to be the girlfriend you show off, the one you hold a little closer in public, but also the bestfriend you can sit with in silence, doing absolutely nothing. I want butterflies with you but I love that you also feel like home. With you, I don’t feel like I have to choose between being loved and being understood. I get both. That’s what you get when there dosti in your pyaar.

It’s what makes you stay even when things aren’t ideal. It’s what makes you patient. It’s what makes you choose kindness over ego, understanding over assumptions. Because you’re not just my lover, you’re my teammate. Surface level attraction can fade, moods change, life can get messy, but I know our friendship is strong, it holds everything together. It gives love something to fall back on, like a safety net. It makes sure that even on the days when we don’t feel perfect, we still feel right with each other.

I love that we can talk about anything and everything, or sometimes nothing at all. I love that we can laugh at the most random things and still understand each other in ways no one else does. I love that even in silence, there’s no awkwardness, just comfort. So no matter where life takes us, I hope we never lose this. This friendship, this ease, this feeling of being completely ourselves with each other. Because I don’t just want a love that looks good from the outside, I want something that lasts, something that feels safe and real on the inside too. Which we are.

Cant wait for my love to turn 20, happy almost birthday!!

I love you

Your bestfriend

Geet
